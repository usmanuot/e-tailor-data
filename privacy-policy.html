Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how E-Tailoring ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile and web application.

1. About E-Tailoring

E-Tailoring is a digital tailoring management application designed for tailoring shop owners, tailors, and administrators to manage customers, measurements, orders, and business records efficiently.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Shop information (for Admin accounts)
  • User role (Super Admin, Admin, Tailor)

2.2 Customer & Measurement Data

  • Customer names and contact details
  • Clothing measurements (e.g. shirt, pant, suit, shalwar kameez)
  • Order history and delivery status
  • Payment and due amount records

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data for performance and bug fixing
  • Log data (errors and crash reports)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To manage tailoring shops, customers, and orders
  • To authenticate users and manage roles
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To ensure data security and prevent unauthorized access

4. Data Storage & Security

We use secure cloud services (such as Supabase) to store and manage data. Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Important: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

5. Permissions Used

The app may request certain permissions such as:

  • Internet access – for cloud synchronization
  • Storage access – for saving reports or documents (if applicable)

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality.

6. Data Sharing

Your data is only accessible to authorized users within your tailoring shop and system administrators when required for support or legal reasons.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of incorrect information
  • Request deletion of your account and data

8. Children’s Privacy

E-Tailoring is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
